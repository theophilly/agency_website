About Fieldinsight

Fieldinsight is a customer relationship management solution built for activities in the field to strengthen companies’ bond with their customers. It's a platform that works offline and online helping your team in the field — including, field-sales, customer service and logistics, all work together to ensure customer satisfaction

Mission

To strengthen the relationship between a business and its customers by having its operations in the field be data driven.

Vision

To transition businesses in Africa to having organized data about their operations in the field.
